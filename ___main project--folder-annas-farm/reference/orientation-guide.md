# Site Orientation & Directional Reference

**Project:** Anna's Farm — Common Space Entry Landscape
**Last updated:** 2026-04-15 (Session 4)

---

## Cardinal Orientation

Per client directive, the primary design orientation is **northwesterly** with the following specifics:

- **More west than north** — the formations lean into the afternoon/evening light
- Sidewalk edge = north reference
- Primary viewing angle approaches from the south/southeast (arriving foot traffic)

## Flat Iron Groupings

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Grouping sizes | 2, 3, or 4 flat irons per grouping |
| Quantity | Non-deterministic — field-determined based on weight, scale, and available material |
| Material | Colorado Red flagstone slabs, installed upright at varied heights and tilts |
| Tilt direction | Northwest (more west than north) |
| Arrangement | Asymmetric, odd-numbered clusters preferred |

## Site 1 — Common Space Entry A (3,500 sqft)

- **Centroid:** 40.000458°N, -105.110036°W
- **Elevation:** ~5,360 ft
- **Perimeter:** 230 ft
- **Shape:** Irregular polygon
- **Backdrop:** Stucco wall (back of frame), distant cottonwoods, Flatirons visible on horizon
- **Key features:**
  - Series of Flatirons-inspired groupings as mountain backdrop
  - Dry creek bed running diagonally through composition
  - Canyon cliff walls (Colorado red strip stone) at creek bends
  - Six tons of boulders in asymmetric clusters
  - Ten solar path lights along curved implied walkway

## Site 2 — Common Space Entry B (1,500 sqft)

- **Centroid:** 40.001489°N, -105.109675°W
- **Elevation:** ~5,350 ft
- **Perimeter:** 194 ft
- **Shape:** Narrower elongated polygon
- **Backdrop:** Stucco wall, split-rail fence, open prairie beyond
- **Key features:**
  - Focused version of Site 1 concept — Flatirons-inspired groupings (compact ridge)
  - Simpler dry creek bed path
  - Four tons of boulders in asymmetric clusters
  - Ten solar path lights in straight line along long axis

## Compass Rose for Prompts

When specifying camera angles in prompts:

| Direction | Meaning in Site Context |
|-----------|------------------------|
| North | Sidewalk / street edge |
| South | Interior / approach direction |
| Northwest | Primary formation tilt direction |
| Southwest | Afternoon golden-hour light source |
| East | Morning light source |

## Lighting & Time of Day

| Shot | Sun Position | Shadow Direction | Notes |
|------|-------------|-----------------|-------|
| Before (midday) | Overhead, slightly camera-left | Short, hard-edged | Neutral documentation |
| After (midday) | Overhead, slightly camera-left | Short, hard-edged | Matched to before |
| Hero (golden hour) | Low, back-left or back-right | Long, soft-edged | Raking light on formations |
| Dusk (blue hour) | Below horizon | None (ambient sky) | Solar lights as primary warm source |

## Existing Site Conditions

- Metal edging already installed at site boundaries
- Drip lines present (existing irrigation infrastructure)
- Prairie dog activity — mulch use is conditional; may attract digging
- Adjacent vegetation: cottonwood, blue spruce, scrub oak, prairie grasses
