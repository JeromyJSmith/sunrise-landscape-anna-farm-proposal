# Sunrise Landscape Co. — Logo Generation Prompts

**Company:** Sunrise Landscape Co.
**Owner:** William Held
**Location:** Colorado
**Industry:** Landscape hardscape — desert xeriscape, rock, boulder, flagstone work

---

## Brand Direction Notes

Colorado-based landscape company doing hardscape work in the Front Range area. The name "Sunrise" is the core visual hook — morning light over landscape. Should feel professional, trustworthy, established — not clip-art, not overly corporate. Think: a contractor you'd hand $33K to without hesitation.

---

## Prompt Concepts

### 1. Mountain Sunrise Mark
> Professional logo for "Sunrise Landscape Co." — a minimalist Colorado mountain ridgeline silhouette with a half-sun rising behind the peak, casting warm gradient rays. Sun in warm amber-to-gold (#D4890B to #F2C94C). Mountains in deep charcoal or forest green. Clean sans-serif typography beneath. Flat vector style, no gradients on the type. White background. Scalable to favicon size.

### 2. Horizon Stone
> Logo for "Sunrise Landscape Co." — a single weathered boulder or stacked flagstone in the foreground with a stylized sunrise arc behind it. Earthy warm palette: sandstone tan, terracotta, warm gold sunrise. Company name in a sturdy slab-serif font. Feels grounded, literally — rock is the product. Vector, clean edges, works on dark and light backgrounds.

### 3. Sun + Shovel Monogram
> Monogram-style logo mark for "Sunrise Landscape Co." — the letter S formed by a curved shovel handle, with a sunrise arc completing the top of the S. Warm copper/gold tones. Accompanying wordmark in clean modern sans-serif. Professional contractor aesthetic, not playful. SVG-ready vector.

### 4. Colorado Dawn
> Logo for "Sunrise Landscape Co." — Colorado Front Range landscape vignette: low foothills silhouette, scattered boulders, sunrise glow on the horizon. Warm Colorado palette: dusty gold, terracotta, sage green, charcoal. Modern flat illustration style. Company name in a geometric sans-serif below.

### 5. Clean Wordmark + Sun Icon
> Minimalist professional logo for "Sunrise Landscape Co." — clean wordmark in a medium-weight sans-serif (think Outfit or Montserrat). A small circular sun icon with radiating lines sits to the left or above the "S" in Sunrise. Color: single warm gold accent (#C8843E copper or #D4890B amber) on white/charcoal. No illustration — pure typography + icon. Versatile for business cards, truck wraps, proposals.

### 6. Stacked Rock Cairn
> Logo for "Sunrise Landscape Co." featuring a stacked rock cairn (3-4 balanced stones) with sunrise rays emanating from behind the top stone. Natural earthy tones. Communicates balance, craftsmanship, natural materials. Clean vector, modern but not trendy. Company name stacked below in two lines: "SUNRISE" bold, "LANDSCAPE CO." lighter weight.

## Generation Notes

- Generate at high resolution (1024×1024 minimum)
- Request "vector style" or "flat design" to get clean edges
- Run each concept in both **light background** and **dark background** variants
- After picking a direction, refine with inpainting to clean up type (AI-generated typography almost always needs manual cleanup or replacement)
- Final production logo should be redrawn in Figma/Illustrator from the AI output — use the AI version as a comp, not the deliverable
