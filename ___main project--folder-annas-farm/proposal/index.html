Professional landscape hardscape installation across two common-space entry areas at Anna's Farm, Lafayette, Colorado. Scope includes full site demolition, grading, weed-fabric underlayment, decorative decomposed granite ground cover (3/4"–1½"), accent river cobble (2"–6"), Colorado moss-rock boulder placement, Lyons sandstone flagstone accents, and solar path lighting — delivering a low-water, HOA-compliant Front Range xeriscape with year-round curb appeal.
