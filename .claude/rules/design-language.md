# Anna's Farm Design Language — non-negotiable

## Materials (Colorado-sourced ONLY)
| Material | Description | Color range |
|---|---|---|
| Colorado red flagstone | Upright slabs, Flatirons-inspired | #c05227 to #9e4324 (warm red-brown) |
| Colorado red strip stone | Shorter vertical strips, 28" tall, 6" thick | #7a5232 (warm brown) |
| Native Colorado granite | Large anchor boulders | #8a8a87 to #d9d6cf (gray-white) |
| River rock / cobble | Dry creek bed fill | #bcb39e (tan-gray) |
| Gravel | Ground cover | #9e8e6b (sandy) |

**Never** use Arizona sandstone, imported marble, or any non-Colorado material.

## Slab orientation
- Flatiron slab groupings face **northwest** (more west than north)
- This mimics the real Flatirons' geological tilt
- North axis = sidewalk direction on site

## Language (non-deterministic)
All proposals, descriptions, and UI labels use flexible language:
- "a series of Flatirons-inspired groupings" NOT "5 slabs in a row"
- "groupings of upright stone forms" NOT "a single formation"
- "along the creek bend" NOT "at coordinates X,Y"

## Lighting intent
- Warm up-lighting behind slabs (2700K, amber glow)
- Bollard pathway lights (3000K, subtle)
- Golden hour (4:30 PM) as the default scene time
- Night mode shows the lighting design at its best
